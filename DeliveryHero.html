Delivery Hero

Delivery Hero is the leading global online food ordering and delivery marketplace with number one market positions in terms of restaurants, active users, gross merchandise value or website traffic, in more countries than any of its competitors. We are present in more than 40 countries around 5 continents. We have strong local teams, who are familiar with all the local pelicularities. Food is not just any product, it is rooted in the culture of every country. In this sense you might see us as the ‘United Nations of Food Delivery’. What makes us special? It’s the way we combine food and tech. Our local teams are working closely together with our central teams that are on the forefront of innovation, e.g. in logistics, in exploring new kitchen concepts, in finding smart way of using data. Did you know, for instance, that our data analysis is enabling restaurants all around the world to improve their service significantly for the benefit of our customers? Currently we have 6,000+ employees globally (plus thousands of employed riders), with 1,000+ staff members working in our Berlin headquarters – all with the mission to create an amazing takeaway experience.
